Reframe a negative thought

Challenge worry with curiosity. Your thoughts are not facts.

Step 1 — Name it

Write the thought that's been on your mind. Don't judge it — just let it out.

Step 2 — Explore it

Your thought:

Choose a question:

—Is there actual evidence this is true, or am I predicting?
—What would I say to a friend who had this thought?
—Have I handled hard things before? What happened?
—What is the most realistic outcome — not best or worst?
